About

Museum of Borderlands is a place for contemplating contemporary fears and monstrosities. It was born as a result of AI hype, feelings of isolation, conspiracy theories and compulsive handwashing. Our museum explores the role machine learning has in detecting deviances – thus, in making of monsters.

Our current collection is spread on the wall next to you. It shows how two invisible monsters, viruses and surveillance, are depicted in an image bank context. These representations often end up illustrating daily news feeds. However, a monster is something yet-to-be – something that always escapes our definition.

The classifications on the wall don’t necessarily reflect the way you see the world. Therefore we ask for your help. You can begin by exploring our current classification system by putting images under the camera on the shelf. You will hear almost real monster narratives based on Reddit and Twitter. After that, you can redefine the categories by putting images into archive boxes where you think they belong. We will retrain our algorithmic eye according to this new information. Thank you!
